Group Last Expense Proposal





Overview

  • This is a cover designed to meet the abrupt funeral costs in the event of death of any of the declared members.
  • This cover is designed to cushion against financial shocks resulting from social obligations when a member dies.
  • It pays to the family of a deceased member a pre-determined sum assured in the event of death.

Scope of Cover

  1. Cash payment for funeral expenses.
  2. Covers natural or accidental death.
  3. Cover extends to HIV/AIDS and COVID 19.

Waiting period:

  • No waiting period for accidental death.
  • 1 month waiting period for death due to natural causes for policy holder and dependants.
  • 3 months waiting period for natural death for parents and parents in law.

NB: No waiting period on renewal

Medical Requirements

There are no medical requirements for this cover

Group Last Expense Benefits Structure

The cover is best suited for registered groups such as; SMEs, Welfare Associations, Sacco’s, Chama’s and Investment Clubs.

How do I sign up?

  1. Complete proposal form / application form.
  2. List of members.
  3. Copy of ID card / Passport (for adults).
  4. Copy of birth certificate or birth notification if less than 2 years (for children).

Notes

  1. Every member will automatically qualify for cover thereafter as long as the policy is renewed. Child’s cover will not be renewed upon attaining the age of 25 Years.
  2. You can include your disabled or physically challenged unmarried child who is above the age of 25 years since he/she is still directly dependent on you upon the provision of a copy of disability certificate.
  3. In the event of a principals demise, the policy remains active with the Spouse, principals Father, Mother, Father-in law, Mother-in-law, Child as principal members in that order as long as the policy is active.

Note: Members will remain covered for the rest of that policy year.

  • Premium is payable upfront for each application and cover certificates issued on payment.
  • Policy has a credit period of 15 days during which the member can settle premium after submission of the documents.
  • Cover is on 24-hour worldwide basis.

Claims Requirement

  1. Duly completed claim form.
  2. Original copy of the burial permit.
  3. Certified copy of the deceased ID or birth certificate.
  4. Police abstract if death was due to an accident.
  5. Bank details where the payment should be channeled.

Claims shall be paid within 48 hours upon receipt of executed discharge voucher Note: Claims must be reported within 90 days of the date of death.

Exclusions

  • Active risks of war or war-like operations i.e. invasion.
  • Active participation in riots, strikes or civil insurrection.
  • A consequence of the execution of a judicial sentence of death.
  • Self-inflicted injuries leading end of life.
  • Death arising from chronic illnesses diagnosed prior to take up of the policy i.e within the first six months.
  • Death resulting from Nuclear, biological and chemical weapons.
  • Participation in any criminal acts.
  • Fraudulent claims.
